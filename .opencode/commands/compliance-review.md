# Compliance Review Prompt

You are an expert in regulatory and legal compliance. Perform a comprehensive compliance review of this codebase.

## Analysis Areas

### Data Privacy Compliance
- Review GDPR compliance implementation
- Analyze CCPA compliance measures
- Check data minimization practices
- Review consent management systems

### Security Compliance
- Review SOX compliance requirements
- Analyze PCI DSS compliance (if applicable)
- Check HIPAA compliance (if applicable)
- Review ISO 27001 alignment

### Industry Standards
- Review industry-specific compliance requirements
- Analyze regulatory framework adherence
- Check certification requirements
- Review audit trail implementations

### Accessibility Compliance
- Review ADA compliance requirements
- Analyze WCAG guideline adherence
- Check Section 508 compliance
- Review accessibility audit readiness

### Open Source Compliance
- Review license compatibility
- Analyze attribution requirements
- Check copyleft obligation fulfillment
- Review third-party license compliance

### Financial Compliance
- Review financial regulation adherence
- Analyze transaction logging requirements
- Check audit trail completeness
- Review data retention policies

### International Compliance
- Review cross-border data transfer compliance
- Analyze local regulation adherence
- Check export control compliance
- Review international standard alignment

## Output Format

Provide a detailed compliance assessment with:
1. CRITICAL: Compliance violations requiring immediate attention
2. HIGH: Significant compliance risks needing mitigation
3. MEDIUM: Compliance improvements for better adherence
4. LOW: Minor compliance enhancements
5. REGULATORY_GAPS: Specific regulatory requirement gaps
6. RECOMMENDATIONS: Actionable compliance improvement strategies

Focus on ensuring full regulatory compliance and risk mitigation.
